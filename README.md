# 🚍 RoadGuard AI

### AI-Powered Smart Driver Safety & Route Monitoring System

RoadGuard AI is an AI-powered transportation safety system designed to reduce road accidents by monitoring driver behavior and road conditions in real time.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Road accidents frequently occur due to:

- Driver drowsiness
- Mobile phone usage while driving
- Poor night visibility
- Animals crossing roads
- Potholes and road hazards

Current safety systems address only individual problems rather than providing a complete intelligent safety solution.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

RoadGuard AI combines Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to:

- Detect driver drowsiness
- Detect mobile phone usage
- Enhance night visibility
- Detect animals on roads
- Detect potholes and road hazards
- Provide instant audio and vibration alerts
